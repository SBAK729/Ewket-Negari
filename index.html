About

Ewket Negari was born from a simple tutoring effort during university, where we saw firsthand how structured support could boost student success. Inspired by that experience and the impact of digital tools like quiz channels we set out to build something bigger. Many Ethiopian high school students struggle to prepare for national exams, not just with content but also with study strategies. Our platform provides structured courses, exam prep tools, and personalized learning support for grades 9 to 12, all aligned with the Ethiopian curriculum. We're here to make academic success accessible for every student.
