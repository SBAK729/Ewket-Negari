About

Ewket Negari was born from a simple tutoring effort during university, where we saw firsthand how structured support could boost student success. Inspired by that experience and the impact of digital tools like quiz channels we set out to build something bigger. Many Ethiopian high school students struggle to prepare for national exams, not just with content but also with study strategies. Our platform provides structured courses, exam prep tools, and personalized learning support for grades 9 to 12, all aligned with the Ethiopian curriculum. We're here to make academic success accessible for every student.

FAQs

Who is Ewket Negari for?

Ewket Negari is designed for Ethiopian high school students in grades 9 to 12 who want to strengthen their understanding of subjects, prepare for national exams, and improve study habits.

What subjects are covered on the platform?

We provide structured courses and learning materials based on the Ethiopian curriculum, including core subjects like Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Civics, and more.

How does the platform help with exam preparation?

Students get access to past exam questions, quizzes, and personalized progress tracking. The system gives feedback and study tips based on performance to help students focus on areas they need to improve.

Is Ewket Negari free to use?

We offer free access to essential learning materials, quizzes, and exam prep tools. In the future, we may introduce affordable premium features to support sustainability, but the core learning experience will remain accessible to all students.
